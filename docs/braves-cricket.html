Braves Cricket required a bold visual identity to stand out in the global T10 cricket league and unify its presence across teams and continents.

Following a change in ownership, the franchise was preparing for major international exposure. My role focused on bringing the new brand to life through uniforms, a team bus wrap, and social media graphics that delivered consistency and energy across every touchpoint.

Client
Braves Cricket

Team
Corban Keating, SD Jake Pearce, JGD

What we delivered
Uniform Design Bus Wrap Social Media Graphics

Production-Ready Apparel Templates

Detailed on-field kits, training gear, and merchandise were designed for Braves Cricket, using manufacturer templates to ensure accurate, print‑ready files.

Results

Braves Cricket led the Abu Dhabi T10 in social media engagement and featured prominently during the 2023 Zimbabwe T10, which reached over 400 million global viewers. The branding helped position Braves as a standout franchise during the league’s rapid international expansion.
